### A - Trap (tripwire)

This trap triggers a [Fog Cloud spell](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Fog_Cloud)

* **Wis (DC 12) Perception** to detect the trap
* **Dex (DC 15)** to disarm. Thieves tools adds prof. Failure at DC10 triggers the trap
